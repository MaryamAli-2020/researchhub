Desktop Companion

Reeza Buddy keeps your tasks softly in view.

A tiny bottom-corner desktop friend for Reeza that signs into your account, pulls your open tasks, and turns them into gentle reminders.

Have some water. Your little desktop friend

What it does

  • Lives in the corner of your desktop without taking over your screen.
  • Signs into the same Reeza account and shows your open tasks.
  • Offers a calmer, more personal reminder experience than a full app window.

Download options

  • Installer: best for most people, with shortcuts and a normal install flow.
  • Portable: launch it directly without installing.
  • Windows: the current packaged build targets Windows x64.

How releases work

New Windows builds are attached automatically whenever a GitHub Release is published. The buttons on this page always point to the latest release files.
